    More Than Just A Chocolate

    Hershey’s Chocolate Company was founded in 1894 by Milton S. Hershey and his brother, William Kidd Hershey. The company began as an ice cream stand on Market Street in Philadelphia. In 1900, they opened their first factory to produce chocolate. Today, Hershey’s operates more than 70 manufacturing facilities worldwide and employs over 6,000 people.

    Ingredients

    Our ingredient list is simple: high quality cocoa beans, sugar, milk powder, vanilla extract, emulsifiers and natural flavor. We use high quality ingredients that are sourced from around the globe. Our cocoa beans come from countries like Ghana, Brazil, Indonesia, and Mexico. These beans are carefully selected for their flavor, texture, and nutritional value.
